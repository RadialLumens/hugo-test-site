By AuthorHere

May 1, 2023

Example post to show how overflow posts are handled on the front page. You will notice that the content written here is not displayed on the front page rather only the title itself. This post was created on 2023-May-5, but was modified to show as the first post in the site history when viewing the Recent Posts section on the front page.

To modify the amount of recent posts that are shown in detail on the front page, change the following variable found in the config.toml:

[params]
  recent_posts_number = #
